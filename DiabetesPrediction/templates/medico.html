About Our Project

With the modernization in lifestyle of human beings especially in urban areas there is a lack in physical activities, therefore diseases related to diabetes have become more common nowadays. Diabetes causes millions of deaths every year. With such a large number of people infected with this disease we need to come up with a possible solution. Problems related to this diseases are not detected in early stages as they will be functioning normally even if they are partially damaged. Our model will help us to predict the risk of this diseases beforehand, which will increase the survival rate of patients.

We have experienced that many people do their regular blood test but are unaware of the risk of disease, health parameters that may be coming on the boundary in their blood report.

We will also be providing advice on the precations you need to take according to your risk , so you can maintain your health.
